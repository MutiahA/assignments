Different cakes with pictures and how to make them

Hi guys, welcome back to my cake blog,I'm excited to share some recipes of different cakes I've tried in the past

Chocolate? Red velvet? Tiramisu? Here are some recipes of most of my go to and easy to make cakes. Each of these baked deserts has not only distinct ingredients, but a method all on it's own. Learn the different types so you can always have varieties of options whenever you feel in the mood to bake.

Recipes

  1. Chocolate cake
    2. About: Chocolate is often the base cake for many other chocolate cakes. It is a common sponge cake with cocoa powder added to it while whisking the ingredients together. Ganache, fudge, sweeteners,and vanilla creme are often used in between two layers as icing.

    Chocolate Cake

  2. Cheesecake
    3. About: Cheesecake has a base made from crushed cookies, which is then topped with thick luscious soft cheese, eggs, and sugar.

    Cheesecake

  3. Carrot cake
    4. About: Cheesecake has a base made from crushed cookies, which is then topped with thick luscious soft cheese, eggs, and sugar

    Carrot cake

  4. Tiramisu cake
    5. About: Tiramisu is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert. It is made of ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa. The recipe has been adapted into many varieties of cakes and other desserts.

    Tiramisu cake

  5. Red velvet
    6. About: The red velvet cake is a simple sponge cake infused with beet root juice and cocoa powder, but nowadays, food color is used instead of beet root, which gives it its distinct maroon color. The cake is topped with a thick white frosting

    Red velvet cake
